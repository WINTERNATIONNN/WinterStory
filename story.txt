Once upon a time, in the school of University of Wisconsin Madison, snow storm happened really often. There was a rumor spread aroud the town saying that something bad would happen during the snow storm.

It was said that during the snow storm, students would go missing at hands of one of UW's own students. THe police could not make a list of suspects. And everyone who encountered the mystery could not describe the event.

This has been going on for years upon years now. Then came along a student named Patrick. He was going to be the change that the university needed. He was going to find the cause of this great mystery and help countless families finally get closure.
